what is a responsive site, and why is responsiveness important?

a responsive site is a webpage that will look swish across platforms; the styles and layout will adjust across different screen resolutions and browser types, including changing browser window size.


what is mobile first design, and why is it important?

'mobile first design' is the design process of creating your layout with mobile devices in mind first and then building into designs for larger screen types.


what are frameworks, what are their pros and cons?

a 'framework' is a particular pattern of coding. pros of using a framework are convenience and consistency, The cons of using frameworks are that we run the risk of over-standardising and can limit creativity.


What is a wireframe and why would we use it?

a wireframe is a mock-up of the website we are to build, typically you would create one at the beginning of a project. It is a useful visual tools to see how the content will juggle before we start building.
